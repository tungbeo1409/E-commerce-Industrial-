Laminar Flow Hood
The laminar flow hood, also known as cleanroom bench or laminar flow workstation, is a piece of equipment used in laboratories or cleanrooms to provide a controlled and sterile environment for various applications. Here are some key points about laminar flow hoods:
  1. The laminar flow hood provided a sterile environment for culturing bacteria without the risk of contamination.
  2. The horizontal laminar flow hood was used for assembly of delicate electronic components, ensuring a particle-free environment.
  3. In the pharmaceutical laboratory, the laminar flow hood was utilized for the aseptic preparation of injectable medications.
  4. The vertical laminar flow hood with UV sterilization allowed for safe manipulation of DNA samples, preventing any external contamination.
